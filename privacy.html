Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

At IntelliWiz Technologies ("we," "our," or "us"), we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application, Sync Party, handles information when you use our services. We do not sell your data to third parties.

2. Data We Collect

Sync Party is designed to function with minimal data collection. We categorize data collection as follows:

  • Local Network Data: To synchronize music between devices, the app requires access to your Local Network. This is used solely to discover and connect to nearby devices. We do not store or transmit this data to our servers.
  • Media Metadata: The app accesses the titles of the MP3 files you select for playback to sync them across devices. This information remains local to your device session.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous diagnostic data (e.g., crash reports) via Apple’s standard App Store tools to help us improve the app.

3. Device Permissions and Local Connectivity

To provide the core functionality of synchronized music playback, "Sync Party" requires access to specific hardware features on your iOS device. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency regarding these permissions.

3.1 Local Network Access

  • Purpose: The App uses Apple's Multipeer Connectivity framework to discover other devices running "Sync Party" nearby.
  • Usage: This permission is strictly used to identify valid Host or Guest devices to establish a connection session.
  • Data Handling: No data regarding your local network structure, other devices, or internet traffic is collected, stored, or transmitted to external servers.

3.2 Wi-Fi and Bluetooth

  • Purpose: These radio technologies are used to establish a direct, peer-to-peer connection between devices.
  • Usage:
    • Bluetooth: Used to broadcast and scan for nearby sessions with low power consumption.
    • Wi-Fi: Used to transfer audio files and synchronization commands between connected devices with high speed and low latency.
  • Location Data: While these technologies can technically be used for location estimation, Sync Party does not access, track, or store your geographic location. The use of Wi-Fi and Bluetooth is limited exclusively to local data transfer.

3.3 Data Persistence

  • Audio Files: Audio files shared during a session are stored in a temporary directory on the Guest device. These files are automatically deleted when the user disconnects or leaves the session. They are not added to the user's permanent music library or backed up to any cloud service.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or as necessary to provide essential app services (such as through Apple’s integrated systems).

5. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal identifiers like your name or email within the app, there is no personal data to delete. If you have contacted us via email for support, you may request the deletion of that correspondence at any time.
